# Roadmap

## Stage 1 - Basic Skeleton

Create the module structure, C# project, `SubModule.xml`, build script, and safe first-load logging.

## Stage 2 - Logging And Safe Execution

Expand logging only as needed and use `SafeExecutor` around future campaign systems.

## Stage 3 - Party Strength Evaluation

Add read-only evaluation for party strength, troop quality, wounded troops, prisoners, speed, and supplies.

## Stage 4 - Settlement Threat Evaluation

Estimate threats to towns, castles, villages, and nearby allied or hostile forces.

## Stage 5 - Lord Logic

Prototype small decision helpers for lord movement and priorities, with detailed logging before any behavior override.

## Stage 6 - Army Logic

Improve army target choice, reinforcement decisions, cohesion handling, and retreat or regroup behavior.

## Stage 7 - Sieges And Garrisons

Improve siege commitment, siege cancellation, garrison priorities, and defensive response.

## Stage 8 - Diplomacy

Evaluate war pressure, border safety, faction exhaustion, and strategic priorities.

## Stage 9 - MCM Settings And Russian Localization

Add MCM settings and Russian localization after stable gameplay systems exist.
